Dress Code Policy

The university requires formal or business casual attire for all students. Ensuring appropriate dress is essential for maintaining a professional academic environment. Here are the key guidelines:

2013 Revised Dress Code Policy

Starting September 07, 2023, the following dress code regulations are in effect:

Uniforms and Dress Code

Effective September 18, 2023, all bona fide TUP students including freshmen who enter the University premises should be in their official uniform except on Wednesdays, Saturdays, and Sundays. Students should purchase their uniforms only from providers allowed by the University. The official uniform bears the TUP school logo.

Specific guidelines based on SOGIE are provided for uniform wear. On Wednesdays, Saturdays, and Sundays, students may enter the university in casual attire, provided they fulfill the guidelines for casual attire.

Parameters on Wearing Casual Attires

  • No shorts, ripped jeans, open footwear, sleeveless shirts, piercings, or crop tops are permitted.
  • Pencil cuts and dresses are permitted for female gender-based wear, provided that the hem is not cut above the knee.
  • Male gender-based students are not permitted to wear earrings.

PE Days

Students are only permitted to wear P.E uniforms during the specified date of their P.E.

Haircut Parameters

All BONA FIDE TUP students must wear clean and acceptable hairstyles when accessing university grounds. Specific rules apply regarding hair length, style, and color.
