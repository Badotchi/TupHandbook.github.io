Latest Memorandum

Dress Code Update

In consideration of the high heat index being experienced in Metro Manila and nationwide and in order to ensure the comfort and safety of the students during this difficult times, the request of the TUP Federation of Student Governments headed by the Office of the Student Regent for students of the TUP System to wear civilian clothing in lieu of student uniform was granted, subject to strict compliance with the approved guidelines.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately and will remain in force until the end of the current semester or until the intense heat has subsided.

Please be guided accordingly.

Allowed Clothing:

  • TOPS: T-SHIRTS, ORGANIZATION SHIRT, SLEEVELESS
  • BOTTOMS: SWEAT PANTS, JOGGING PANTS, JEANS
  • SHORTS AND SKIRTS (length is maximum of 2 inches above the knee)
  • FOOTWEAR: Any form of enclosed footwear

Not Allowed:

  • TOPS: TUBES, SPAGHETTI, SEE THROUGH, CROPPED TOPS
  • BOTTOMS: FITTED SHORT SKIRTS, SHORT SHORTS, LEGGINGS, RIPPED JEANS
  • FOOTWEAR: SANDALS, SLIPPERS, CROCS

For more information, visit TUP Manila USG Facebook Page.
